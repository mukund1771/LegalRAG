SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT (EXHIBIT A)

This Service Level Agreement ("SLA") supplements the Master Vendor Services Agreement between Acme Corp and Vendor XYZ Inc.

1. Uptime Commitment
Vendor commits to a monthly uptime of 99.9% ("Availability Target") for the hosted services, measured on a calendar-month basis and excluding scheduled maintenance windows.

2. Measurement
Availability is calculated as the total minutes in the month minus downtime minutes, divided by the total minutes in the month, expressed as a percentage.

3. Service Credits
If monthly uptime falls below the Availability Target, Customer shall be entitled to service credits as the sole and exclusive remedy: 10% of monthly fees for availability between 99.0% and 99.9%, 25% for availability between 95.0% and 99.0%, and 50% for availability below 95.0%.

4. Chronic Failure
If Vendor fails to meet the Availability Target for three (3) consecutive months, Customer may terminate the Master Vendor Services Agreement for cause without penalty.

5. Exclusions
Downtime caused by Customer's own systems, force majeure events, or scheduled maintenance notified at least 48 hours in advance shall not count toward downtime.
